Jonita Gandhi (born October 23, 1989) is a Canadian singer known for her work in the Indian film and music industries. She has recorded songs predominantly in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu languages with few in Punjabi, Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali. Some of her most acclaimed songs include "The Breakup Song", "Mental Manadhil". She is also well known for her YouTube presence. Her singing debut started through Chennai Express in 2013.
